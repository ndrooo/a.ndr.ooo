# About me...

([Disambiguation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Disambiguation_(disambiguation)))

## The human being {#human}
I'm **Andrew**, a software developer and curious person in New York City. For my job I build **websites** and I am particularly focused on making them **accessible**. When I feel like writing software for fun I build **different websites**, dabble in **game development**, mess around with **Unix**, and continuously improve my [NixOS configuration](https://github.com/ndrooo/nix).

I am a **gardener** at my community garden and at home. I'm interested in **ecology**, particularly of plants and trees. I know a lot about **tea** and I like to drink it too. I dabble in **electronics and fabrication** and have built a few keyboards and some smaller projects. I'm very interested in **home electrification** and **climate tech**.

You can contact me at [a@ndr.ooo](mailto:a@ndr.ooo).

## The website {#website}
**Hello, this is the website speaking.** I am built in [11ty](https://www.11ty.dev/) using a little bit of [WebC](https://www.11ty.dev/docs/languages/webc/). I am built entirely by the aforementioned **human being**, mostly within [VSCode](https://code.visualstudio.com/) on devices running [NixOS](https://nixos.org/). I am hosted on [Cloudflare Workers](https://www.cloudflare.com/products/workers/). My **body font** is [Atkinson Hyperlegible](https://www.brailleinstitute.org/freefont/) and my **heading font** is [Fraunces](https://fraunces.undercase.xyz/). The color scheme is called Sumac.

The statement above this one is a [colophon](https://indieweb.org/colophon), which explains how I was made in case you want to **build something similar**. If you do, take a look at the [website resources here](/web/) and learn about [how to make your website accessible](/accessibility/). You can **copy** whatever you want from me because I am [MIT licensed](https://github.com/ndrooo/a.ndr.ooo/blob/main/LICENSE) and my **source code** is available on [GitHub](https://github.com/ndrooo/a.ndr.ooo).
