The website you're on right now is a digital garden! It's a place to put my public notes and thoughts on many different topics. Every note is an evolving document, so as I think or learn more about a topic I may develop, rewrite, amend, or extend the relevant note. A garden is based on [[Hypertext|hypertext]], which is to say it embraces the idea fundamental to the web of a collection of pages (or thoughts) networked together by links. Sometimes links will point to pages that don't yet exist, but this is on purpose! See my [[404 page]] for more.
